About

A bit
about me.

I'm a recent graduate based in Philadelphia with a background in IT support — which is actually where I got hooked on UX. Watching people struggle with software that should've been easier to use made me want to fix things from the start, not after the fact.

I approach problems by talking to people first. I like understanding the "why" before jumping to solutions — and I can code, which means I think about what's actually buildable while I design.

I'm looking for entry-level roles in product design or product development where I can learn fast, contribute early, and help build things people genuinely enjoy using.
