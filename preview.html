About Me

I'm a UX designer with a background in IT support at ICON PLC, where I saw firsthand how good (and bad) software design impacts people's daily work. That experience sparked my transition into UX—I wanted to be on the front lines of creating better experiences, not just fixing broken ones.

I approach design through a research-first lens, combining user insights with technical feasibility. My background in IT gives me a unique advantage—I understand both what users need and what's technically possible, which helps me design solutions that actually ship.

I'm seeking entry-level UX roles where I can contribute both design thinking and technical skills. I'm especially excited about products in healthcare, education, or sustainability—domains where thoughtful design can make a real difference in people's lives.
